Thank you for trying this application.

Press...
W,A,S,D - moves the crosshair pixel by pixel for fine tuning the position.
P - to activate the positionning, then click where you want the crosshair.
O - to open a file (PNG, BMP), alpha channel is supported
R - to reset to default values.

N.B. you have to select the Crosshair app in your taskbar
     after each click since the app is clickthrough.
